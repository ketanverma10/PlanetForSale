Mercury

Mercury is the smallest and fastest planet in the Solar System, orbiting closest to the Sun.

Venus

Earth

Earth is the only known planet to support life, with vast oceans and diverse ecosystems.

Mars

Mars is known as the Red Planet due to its iron-rich dust and is a prime candidate for future colonization.

Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet, with a massive storm known as the Great Red Spot.

Saturn

Saturn is famous for its stunning ring system, made mostly of ice particles.

Uranus

Uranus rotates on its side and has a pale blue appearance due to methane in its atmosphere.

Neptune
